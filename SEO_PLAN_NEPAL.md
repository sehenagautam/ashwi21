# Ashwi Furniture SEO Plan (High-Intent Nepal)

## Objective
Grow high-intent organic traffic in Nepal and increase direct product inquiries from users searching with purchase intent.

## High-Intent Keyword Clusters
- Transactional: `furniture factory nepal`, `buy furniture kathmandu`, `bed price nepal`, `wardrobe price nepal`, `sofa set nepal`
- Commercial intent: `best furniture factory in kathmandu`, `modern furniture nepal`, `mandir furniture nepal`
- Branded/local intent: `ashwi furniture`, `ashwi furniture kathmandu`

## Plan
1. Technical SEO foundation
- Strong homepage metadata (title/description/keywords)
- Open Graph + Twitter tags for social previews
- Canonical URLs
- Structured data (`Organization` + per-product `Product` schema)
- `robots.txt`
- Auto-generated `sitemap.xml` from product catalog on every build

2. On-page SEO implementation
- Route-level SEO metadata for Home, About, Product pages
- Homepage content block targeting Kathmandu/Nepal high-intent terms
- About page trust and local relevance copy
- Product detail SEO title/description per product

3. Conversion-focused SEO
- Keep strong inquiry CTA flow to WhatsApp
- Improve product discoverability through searchable category/subcategory structure
- Focus on terms that indicate buying intent rather than informational traffic only

## Next 30-Day SEO Actions
1. Submit sitemap in Google Search Console
2. Verify indexing of `/`, `/about`, and product pages
3. Add FAQ content for delivery areas, lead time, materials, and payment policy
4. Build dedicated category landing pages for Beds, Wardrobes, Sofas, and Mandir Units
5. Track CTR and refine titles/meta based on Search Console query data
